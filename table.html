Courses and Schools

School Courses
School of Engineering
  • Frontend Engineering: Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and other modern frontend frameworks to build user-friendly interfaces.
  • Backend Engineering: Gain expertise in server-side development with Node.js, Python, databases, and APIs for scalable applications.
  • Cloud Engineering: Master cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud to design, deploy, and manage cloud-based solutions.
  • Cybersecurity: Learn to secure systems and networks, focusing on penetration testing, threat detection, and data protection.
School of Product
  • Product Design: Explore user-centered design principles, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing to create impactful products.
  • Marketing: Learn strategies for digital marketing, branding, SEO, and content creation to promote products effectively.
  • Management: Acquire skills in project management, team leadership, and product lifecycle management for successful project delivery.
School of Data
  • Data Analysis: Learn to analyze and visualize data using tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI to make data-driven decisions.
  • Data Science: Master Python, R, machine learning, and statistical modeling to extract insights from complex datasets.
  • Data Engineering: Build and optimize data pipelines and workflows, working with big data technologies like Hadoop and Spark.
